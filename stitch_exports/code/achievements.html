ACHIEVEMENTS

AAES PEER Grant Award (FY 2026)

Recipient of the award

I am honored to receive the Alabama Agricultural Experiment Station (AAES) Professional Enhancement for Emerging Researchers (PEER) Grant under Group B (Graduate Students) for FY 2026. This competitive award provides $5,000 in funding to support my research project titled "Bio-Oil Spray Atomization and Combustion Analysis for Renewable Aviation Fuel." The project aims to advance sustainable aviation fuel technologies through detailed spray and combustion analysis.

Model Mania Extreme 2025

Winner

I had the exciting opportunity to participate in the Model Mania Extreme Competition, a high-stakes, head-to-head challenge held at the 3DEXPERIENCE World Playground during the annual event at the George R. Brown Convention Center in Houston, Texas. As a Certified SOLIDWORKS Expert (CSWE), I competed against another CSWE in a timed modeling duel where we raced to accurately replicate a complex SOLIDWORKS design. Drawing on advanced skills, precision, and quick decision-making, I completed the model first and emerged as the winner. The competition was an exhilarating experience that pushed my technical abilities under pressure and provided a great platform to engage with the broader SOLIDWORKS community.

Ferrari Trivia Challenge 2025

Winner

I participated in the Ferrari Trivia Challenge, an exciting event organized by HCL CAMWorks at the 3DEXPERIENCE World Playground in the R. George Brown Convention Center, Houston, Texas. The challenge tested participants' knowledge of Ferrari history, engineering, and racing legacy through a series of fast-paced trivia questions. By successfully answering the questions, I emerged as a winner and was thrilled to receive an incredible 1/18 scale model of the Ferrari SF23 as a prize. It was a fun and rewarding experience that combined my passion for engineering with a love for high-performance automotive design.

IUTAA Research Excellence Award 2023

Recipient of the award

One of my proudest achievements to date is receiving the prestigious “IUTAA Research Excellence Award 2023” for outstanding research contributions in my field. This award is given annually by the Islamic University of Technology Alumni Association (IUTAA) to recognize the outstanding achievements of remarkable alumni in the field of innovative research. To be recognized by IUT, a leading engineering educational institution in the country and a breeding ground for brilliant minds who have set exemplary standards in the field of research and innovation worldwide, is truly an honor. IUTAA took the initiative to establish the “IUTAA Research Excellence Awards” to celebrate and recognize the achievements of its alumni, and I am humbled to be among the recipients. The award was presented to me by the honorable Vice Chancellor of Islamic University of Technology, Prof. Dr. Mohammad Rafiqul Islam, and it was a great pleasure and honor to receive it. I never imagined that I would get this award at such an early stage of my career, and to be judged by a panel consisting of the top researchers of Bangladesh makes it even more special. This recognition is a testament to the hard work and dedication that I have put into my research and it serves as a motivation for me to continue to strive for excellence in my field. I am proud to be a part of the IUT community and to have been given the opportunity to receive an education from such a renowned institution.

SolidWorks Champion Program

SolidWorks Champion

One of my greatest achievements is being listed in the top 170 passionate SolidWorks users around the world in the SOLIDWORKS Champions Program. This program, designed by Dassault Systèmes, aims to acknowledge and connect community members from around the world who go above and beyond in sharing their passion and expertise for SOLIDWORKS and 3DEXPERIENCE. Being recognized in this program is a testament to my dedication and expertise in the field of SolidWorks. I am incredibly honored to be among the most passionate users of the software and to be able to share my knowledge and experience with the community. What makes this achievement even more special is that I was able to represent Bangladesh and my alma mater, the Islamic University of Technology (IUT), in this program. IUT is a leading engineering educational institution in the country, and to be able to represent it in an international program is truly an honor. As a SolidWorks Champion, I am part of a community of experts who are dedicated to sharing their knowledge and experience with others. This program allows me to connect with other users from around the world and to learn from their experiences. It also gives me the opportunity to give back to the community by sharing my own expertise and helping others to improve their skills.
