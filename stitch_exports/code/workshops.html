Short Course on 3D Printing Using Reverse Engineering Technique by MPE, IUT

The Mechanical and Production Engineering (MPE) department at IUT organized a dynamic short course on "3D Printing Using Reverse Engineering Technique" in the 17th and 18th of November, 2023. The course garnered enthusiastic participation from diverse industrial backgrounds, fostering an environment of shared learning and innovation. We had an immersive experience covering scanning methods, (FDM) 3D printing, and additive manufacturing techniques. Practical
